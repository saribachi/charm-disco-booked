FAQ

Questions, answered

What is a reading?

A 30-minute call where we read your situation: what is working, what is quietly leaking, and whether outbound is the bottleneck. No remedies until the read is done.

How is this different from hiring an SDR?

An SDR sends volume on whatever script you hand them. We find the message that actually books meetings, what to say and who to say it to, then run the whole system and scale it. You get the right message at scale, not a headcount to build.

How soon do we see pipeline?

Campaigns go live in the first few weeks, once the system is built and the infrastructure is warmed. Pipeline follows as replies start landing and we work them.

Do you work with our CRM and tools?

Yes. Opportunities flow into the system your team already runs on. Light integration comes with Growth, complex integration with Scale.

What about deliverability?

Full at every tier, warmed and monitored, never gated. It is the whole game and we treat it that way.

Is there a long contract?

We keep it flexible. The best monthly rate goes to longer commitments or paying up front, but you are never forced into a long term to start.

What if we are not ready for a full program?

Run a single campaign from $1,000 to see the system work, then decide. We will turn it into a fit conversation on the reading.

Who is Charm not for?

Teams with no clear offer, or no capacity to close the meetings we book. If outbound is not your bottleneck, we will tell you on the read rather than sell you a remedy you do not need.
